Circuit Design

This project is a cellphone detector and signal jammer circuit. I designed this circuit to first test for the presence of a cellphone signal in the GSM bands 850 - 900 MHz. After detection of mobile activty, a signal jammer (noise within the GSM bands) is generated to interefere with cellphone and continues until the presence of the cellphone is gone (no longer picked up by antenna). I built this circuit for fun and to demonstrate my technical knowledge of electromagnetics and electronics.

Matlab & Simulink

This project demonstrates my knowledge of control engineering and uses Matlab and Simulink to do all calculations and simulations. Transfer functions represent a systems relationship of the output to the input. Here I start with a given transfer function (ie system) and show how to deconstruct the function to help gain control of the system. I analyze the time-domain behavior of the uncompensated system by plotting the step response and determining the transient and steady-state properties. I also find the damping ratio, natural and undamped frequency, taking into consideration the overall properties of the system, checking the overall stability, steady state error, overshoot and rise time. This demonstrates my knowledge of how to analyze a given system, reverse engineer, improve a system and predict responses.
